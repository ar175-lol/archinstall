# Q&A

## Q: Why this fork even exist?
The original `archinstall` suffers from a bloated "let's bundle everything" mentality. Official profiles for desktop environments (DE) and window managers (WM) are cluttered with redundant status bars, heavy display managers, ancient 2010s terminals, and gigabytes of useless icon packs and themes.

## Q: Why did you remove some packages that I need?
You can install them, Arch Linux is following KISS philosophy. I'm just removing some "extra" packages.
